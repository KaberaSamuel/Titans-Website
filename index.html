We guide aspiring individuals in the process of growing their talents

Icreate-Rwanda: The Rwanda's Modern Art Training center. We provide professional art programs to our students. We follow a hybrid model where our students spend less time with their coachs, and spend more in their workshops trying new stuffs and practicing what they've learnt. In addition to classroom sessions, each student assigned a mentor who is an expert in the respective field—who we guide the student learning journey. We've also build a gallery dedicated to our students/alumni artworks. Our current programs are about physical art,i.e., Drawing, painting and sculpting. Then we also digital art programs, those are 2D/3D animations and modeling.

At icreate Rwanda we've completely transformed how we approach students across different programs. We don't just teach how to draw, paint or animate, We also teach the right mindset a professional artist should have. We cultivate an imaginative and creative thinking in our students.

About Our Mission

Mission Statement

Our mission is to help aspiring individuals make a successful career in the art industry by providing high-quality trainings and relevant tools for students to practice their craft

Problem Statement

Despite some efforts to promote art talents, The art sector is still not doing great here in Rwanda. There are very few students in Art schools. Many individuals don't even know that art is a skill that can be learn—they think it is some talent you're born with! Even for those students doing art, many of them lack required tools.

Our Solution

To improve the art sector in Rwanda, we will start an art school that offers structured trainings in painting, sculpting, and digital art (3D & 2D CGI). In the first weeks, students will learn basic drawing skills to help them put their ideas on paper. After that, they will choose their preferred art path. We will also make sure they have the required tools for practice.
