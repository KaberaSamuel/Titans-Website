Mission Background

Problem Background

In Rwanda there is a problem of undeveloped TVET sector. Statistics says that only there are only 18% proffesionals in the TVET sector

About Our Mission

As the titans team we want to promote the TVET sector. We want to educate people about the opportunities in the TVET sector and gradually increase number of individuals in it [TVET sector]

How We will Do It

Firstly, we will raise awareness (by campaigns), so that parents and students can know exciting careers in the TVET sector.
Secondly, we will work with the government of Rwanda to find a way to lower the costs of TVET programs, so that more people can afford them.
