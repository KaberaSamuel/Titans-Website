Titans: The Rwanda's Modern Art Training center. We provide professional art programs to our students. We follow a hybrid model where our students spend less time with their coachs, and spend more in their workshops trying new stuffs and practicing what they've learnt. In addition to classroom sessions, each student assigned a mentor who is an expert in the respective fieldâ€”who we guide the student learning journey. We've also build a gallery dedicated to our students/alumni artworks. Our current programs are about physical art,i.e., Drawing, paintings, sculpting Painting and Computer art. Then we also digital art programs, those are 2D & 3D animations and modeling.

At icreate Rwanda we've completely transformed how we approach students across different programs. We don't just teach how to draw, paint or animate, We also teach the right mindset a professional artist should have. We cultivate an imaginative and creative mindset in our students.

Our Programs

Painting Program

At icreate, we provide quality trainings on the modern painting. We provide required tools, i.e., top-notch brushes, drop cloths, roller frames,...

Sculpting Program

At icreate, we breakdown the sculpting process with the help of experts, making it easier for learners. We focus on developing abstraction and imagination in our learners

Digital Art Program

At icreate, we provide both online and onsite CGI programs. The learners are given high-performing computers with dedicated graphics to ensure a smooth workflow
